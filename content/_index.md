# Welcome to DevOpsDays Switzerland 2025

DevOpsDays Switzerland brings together the Swiss DevOps community for two days of learning, networking, and collaboration. Join us for keynotes, talks, ignites, workshops, and open spaces focused on building resilient systems.

## Event Details

**Dates:** June 15-16, 2025  
**Venue:** Swiss Tech Convention Center, Lausanne  
**Expected Attendees:** 300+

## This Year's Theme: Building Resilient Systems

In an increasingly complex technological landscape, building systems that can withstand failures, adapt to change, and recover quickly has become essential. This year's DevOpsDays Switzerland focuses on the practices, tools, and cultural approaches that enable resilient systems.

## What to Expect

- **Keynotes** from industry leaders
- **Technical talks** on cutting-edge practices
- **Ignite talks** for quick insights
- **Hands-on workshops** for practical learning
- **Open spaces** for community-driven discussions
- **Networking** with the Swiss DevOps community
