DevOpsDays Zurich 2026 will be held at the Alte Kaserne in Winterthur.

## Venue

**Alte Kaserne Winterthur**  
Technikumstrasse 8  
8400 Winterthur, Switzerland

[View on Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps?q=Alte+Kaserne,+Technikumstrasse+8,+8400+Winterthur,+Switzerland)

## Getting There

### By Public Transport

The Alte Kaserne in Winterthur is very well accessible by public transport. We recommend using public transport.

**From Zurich Main Station:**

- Take S-Bahn lines S8, S12, S19, or S29 to Winterthur (approximately 25 minutes)
- From Winterthur station, it's a 10-minute walk to the venue

**From Zurich Airport:**

- Direct train connection to Winterthur (approximately 20 minutes)

### By Car

Parking is available but not validated. We strongly recommend using public transport instead.
