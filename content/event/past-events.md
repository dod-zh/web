# Past Events

Explore the history of DevOpsDays Switzerland and see how our community has grown over the years.

## DevOpsDays Switzerland 2024
**June 10-11, 2024 | Geneva**

Theme: "DevOps at Scale"  
Venue: Campus Biotech  
Attendees: 280

Our 2024 event focused on scaling DevOps practices in large organizations. Highlights included keynotes on platform engineering and workshops on Kubernetes at scale.

- 6 workshops
- 12 open spaces

## DevOpsDays Switzerland 2023
**June 12-13, 2023 | Zurich**

Theme: "Culture & Automation"  
Venue: ETH Zurich  
Attendees: 250

Our inaugural Swiss event brought together the local DevOps community for the first time. The focus was on building DevOps culture and practical automation techniques.

[View 2023 Program](/events/2023/) | [Photo Gallery](/events/2023/photos/) | [Videos](/events/2023/videos/)

- 4 workshops
- 10 open spaces
