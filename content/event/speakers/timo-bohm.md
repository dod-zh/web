### Cloud Infrastructure & Delivery Lead

Timo is a Principal Consultant and Team Lead with deep roots in Cloud Infrastructure and Delivery Management. He specializes in bridging the gap between technology, business, and security. Repeatedly reminded that the "Project Mindset" is fundamentally incompatible with reliable operations, he advocates for shifting the focus from temporary fixes to sustainable, long-lived teams.
