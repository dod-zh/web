### Luc Juggery, Developer Relations Engineer, Exoscale

I'm a software engineer with over 20 years of experience in large corporations and startups, including roles as a co-founder. I continuously expand my knowledge through side projects, online courses, and participations in technical events - as both speaker and attendee.

My interest in the container ecosystem has been a significant part of my career, during which I developed expertise in areas such as Docker, Kubernetes, cloud-native applications, DevOps, infrastructure as code, and CI/CD pipelines. I'm passionate about learning, teaching, and sharing knowledge on these technologies.
